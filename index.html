TELL ME MORE...

Beantown Bash is not your average hackathon. Here's a few things that make it special.

  • We don't care if your project has machine learning or some fancy buzzwords. We want you to make something cool and meaningful to you.
  • It's judged by YOU. Yes, you. Your fate won't depend on software developers we've found to look for buzzwords.
  • There's no requirements on what you should make. Preferably, it should be tech-related, but beyond that, the world is your oyster.

WHY?

We've got a mission. We want to make hackathons truly fun and enjoyable. Hackathons are a way to show off your skills, whether that's coding, problem-solving, or project management. They're not just a way to build your resume.

Join us on February 12-14, 2023, for a weekend of fun, hanging out with great people, and building something awesome.
